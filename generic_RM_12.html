✥Unit 12: Project Management and Managing Risk

Outcomes from the Team Exercises and activites

Unit 12: Project Management and Managing Risk aims to equip learners with the essential methodologies and tools for effective project management and risk mitigation in computing projects. Recognizing that each project is unique, with its specific goals and varying environments, this unit underscores the infeasibility of a universal approach to project management. It introduces principal project management methodologies, emphasizing the necessity of selecting appropriate metrics for performance measurement and planning for significant potential risks. Through exploring project life cycles, risk and uncertainty impact, and the dynamics of change management, the unit prepares students to formulate comprehensive risk management plans and adapt to changes proactively, ensuring project quality, budget adherence, and timely completion.
